Common Questions

How does the Smart Routing work? expand_more
Our system detects the star rating chosen by the customer. If it's 4-5 stars, they are automatically prompted to share that review on public sites like Google or Facebook. If it's 1-3 stars, they are given a private feedback form that is sent directly to your management team.
Is there a contract or commitment? expand_more
No. All our plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. We believe in providing value that keeps you with us, not locking you into long-term contracts.
Can I customize the Smile Meter? expand_more
